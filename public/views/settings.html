MOBILE SECURITY

These are things you should do to keep others from invading your privacy:
1. Block outside trackers with epicbrowser.com or set privacy options on current browser.
2. Protect searches with search engines that make search anonymous (e.g. epicsearch.in). Use private-browsing option on drop-down menu to automatically erase.
3. Encrypt your devise with Security for Apple and bitlocker for others.
4. Download a free virtual private network such as spotflux.com or hotspotshield.com.
5. Change Facebook settings to "Friends Only".
6. Clear browser history and cookies regularly.
7. Use an IP Masker on your device.
8. Password protect your device.

If you are not convinced, go to Reddit Relationships and read some personal horror stories!

Also, using free Wi-Fi can put privacy at risk! Here are guidelines to stay connected and reasonably safe while using public networks:

1. Only use sites with https in front of URL.
2. Use a virtual private network, or VPN. No VPN, use Tor you can download at Torproject.org.
3. Sign up for two-step verification at personal user site (e.g., Facebook, Yahoo).
4. Bring only what you need and turn off what is not being used. Consider creating "on-the-go" email and password.

Data pirates go for easy pickings so guard against snatch and grab!
